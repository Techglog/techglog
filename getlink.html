Self Modification

Documents Required for Date of Birth Modification:

  • Digitised NPC Attestation Certificate for those born before 1992.
  • Digitised NPC Birth Certificate for those born after 1992.
Documents Required for Change of Name Modification:
  • Court affidavit.
  • Newspaper extract.
  • Marriage certificate in the case of marital status.
  • Other government ID.
Documents Required for Change of Address Modification:
  • Utility bill.
  • Tenancy agreement.
  • Community leader’s attestation letter.
Documents Required for Phone Number Modification:
  • Police report (provide your phone number without “0” after the “+234” country code, for example +234 812345…).
If you have these documents above, you can proceed with the process. Note: If you don't have these documents and payment is made, you won't get a refund from the website.
